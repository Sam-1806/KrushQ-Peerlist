Detect Your Plant's Disease


Early detection of crop diseases is crucial for successful farming. By carefully observing your plants and recognizing the signs of infection, you can take swift action to prevent further damage and protect your yield.

Apple Scab

Apple scab is a fungal disease that affects apple and crabapple trees, caused by the fungus Venturia inaequalis. 1 It results in olive-green to brown spots on leaves and tan, sunken spots on fruit, potentially leading to premature leaf drop, reduced fruit yield, and lower fruit quality. 1 Effective management strategies include raking and removing fallen leaves in autumn, planting resistant varieties, and applying fungicide sprays at appropriate times during the growing season. 2

  • Symptoms: On Leaves
  • Impact: Fruit Quality
  • Chemical: Fungicides
